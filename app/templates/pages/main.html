Introducing VYGEM,

an innovative application engineered for the valuation of gemstones, addressing the challenge posed by the lack of a standardized pricing system in the gem market. It's important to note that our program calculates price per carat based on prevailing market price trends, rather than a fixed standard. Developed through the collaborative expertise of seasoned gem mologists, coupled with cutting-edge artificial intelligence and real-time gemstone market monitoring, Vygem offers a user-friendly platform suitable for both experienced professionals and newcomers in the realm of precious stone assessment.
